## ‚ùì Anticipated Questions & Answers

**Q: How does the AI detect market events?**
A: The system monitors real-time news feeds, market data, and social media sentiment. When significant events are detected, it analyzes portfolio exposure and generates recommendations within seconds.

**Q: What if the customer disagrees with the recommendation?**
A: Customers can dismiss recommendations or request modifications. The system learns from these interactions to improve future suggestions.

**Q: How is compliance ensured?**
A: Every recommendation goes through automated compliance checks against client risk profiles, position limits, and regulatory requirements before reaching the Investment Officer.

**Q: Can this scale to millions of customers?**
A: Yes, the AI-native architecture is designed for scale. The system can process thousands of portfolios simultaneously and prioritize based on risk exposure.

**Q: What about data privacy and security?**
A: All customer data is encrypted end-to-end. The system complies with GDPR, Hong Kong PDPO, and other relevant regulations. Access is role-based and fully audited.
